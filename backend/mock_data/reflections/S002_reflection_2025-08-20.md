## Lucas's Response

Yesterday in group discussion I felt annoyed when people wanted to change my discussion plan. I spent a long time making the questions and organizing them by theme and then everyone wanted to just talk about random stuff.

I don't really know why it bothered me so much. I guess I felt like my work didn't matter. But also I know that discussions are supposed to be flexible. Ms. Rodriguez reminded me of that.

I'm not really good at talking about feelings. I usually just try to focus on the task and ignore how I feel. That usually works.
